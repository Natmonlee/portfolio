Instructions

Mine Hunter

  • Reveal every safe tile to win.
  • Avoid the mines!
  • Left-click to reveal a tile.
  • Right-click to flag a tile if you suspect there is a mine on it. If you change your mind left-click the tile to uncover it.
  • If the tile shows a number then it is the number of mines that are on adjacent tiles(horizontally, vertically and diagonally)
  • Mine percentage is 14% by default but you can change this, as well as grid size, in a custom game.
  • Choose an option from the "New Game" dropdown to begin!
  • Good luck!
